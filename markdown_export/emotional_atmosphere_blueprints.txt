### 🔵 Blueprint Template 1: EmotionTriggerVolume

**Purpose:** A trigger volume that changes emotional cues (audio, lighting, post-processing) when the player enters or exits.

**Key Components:**
- Box Collision (Trigger)
- Audio Component (optional)
- Post Process Volume (linked or adjusted)
- Blueprint Variables:
  - `EmotionType` (Enum: Calm, Tense, Fear, Wonder, Sadness, etc.)
  - `AmbientAudioCue` (Sound Cue)
  - `LightingProfile` (Custom Struct or Actor Reference)

**Logic Flow:**
1. OnComponentBeginOverlap → Check if overlapping actor is player
2. Trigger:
   - Update global PlayerEmotionManager
   - Play AmbientAudioCue
   - Apply Post-Process/Lighting settings
3. OnComponentEndOverlap → Restore default emotion (if needed)

### 🔵 Blueprint Template 2: PlayerEmotionManager (Actor Component)

**Purpose:** Central component tracking current emotional state, notifying subsystems.

**Variables:**
- `CurrentEmotion` (Enum)
- `EmotionIntensity` (Float 0–1)
- `EmotionChangeSpeed` (Float)
- `LastUpdatedSource` (Actor or Location Reference)

**Functions:**
- `UpdateEmotion(NewEmotion: Enum, Intensity: Float, SourceActor: Actor)`
  - Smoothly transition from old to new state
  - Notify:
    - Audio subsystem (music and ambience)
    - UI or VFX triggers
    - NPC AI (if tied to player proximity)
- `GetCurrentEmotion()`
- `FadeEmotionBackToNeutral(Time: Float)`

**Usage:**
- Attached to PlayerCharacter Blueprint
- Called by EmotionTriggerVolume, Combat Events, Discovery, or Health thresholds

### 🔵 Blueprint Template 3: Dynamic Audio Modulator (Subsystem or Manager Actor)

**Purpose:** Adjust background audio layers based on emotional state.

**Components:**
- AudioManager (singleton or ambient actor in level)
- Arrays of `Sound Cues` grouped by EmotionType

**Functions:**
- `SetEmotionAudio(EmotionType)`
  - Fade out current layer
  - Fade in appropriate ambient/music layers
  - Optionally use RTPC or volume blending

**Inputs:**
- Called by PlayerEmotionManager
- Listens to player state or volume triggers

**Optional Features:**
- Use MetaSounds to blend ambient layers based on `PlayerEmotionManager.EmotionIntensity`
- Reverb/echo zone change when entering fear or awe states
