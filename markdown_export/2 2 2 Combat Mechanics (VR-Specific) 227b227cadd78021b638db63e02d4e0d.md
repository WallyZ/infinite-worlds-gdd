# 2.2.2: Combat Mechanics (VR-Specific)

| Unsafe Motion | Replace with | Integration Style |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Kicking | Left or Right Foot Tap Button | Triggers contextual kick animation; based on player stance, direction |
| Jumping | Stance Shift + Quick Grip Squeeze | Character jumps or vaults automatically |
| Dodging | Head lean or crouch detection | Real-time lean controls sidestep; add slow-mo for clarity |
| Rolling | Double-tap direction or use dash gesture | Animate roll while camera eases into arc |
| Grappling | Use two-handed grab mechanics + physics | Inspired by *Blade & Sorcery*, but with auto-safety limits |
