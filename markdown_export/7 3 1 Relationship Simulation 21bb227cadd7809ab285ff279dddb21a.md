# 7.3.1 Relationship Simulation

- Relationships develop along multiple axes:
    - Trust, affection, fear, jealousy, respect, ideological alignment.
- Romance, rivalry, mentorship, or siblinghood may emerge based on:
    - Shared trauma, player actions, factional pressures, time spent together.
- Includes **relationship thresholds** for major turning points (e.g. confessions, betrayal, commitment).
- Affection, suspicion, rivalry, grudges
- Layers: public face, inner feelings, hidden motives
