# 8.2 Art Style Reference

### 🖌️ Art Style Reference

**🎯 Primary Goal:**

A *hyper-realistic base* grounded in physical accuracy, layered with *stylized fantasy flourishes* that heighten mood, mystery, and magic.

**🎨 Inspirations:**

- *The Witcher 3*, *Kingdom Come: Deliverance* – realistic textures, lighting, armor, and environmental detail
- *Skyrim* (modded) – realism merged with fantasy surrealism
- Historical medieval European art, materials, and architecture
- Natural ecosystems and biome-specific biodiversity

**🧾 Palette & Lighting Philosophy:**

- Base palette rooted in earth tones and natural materials (wood, stone, leather, cloth)
- Fantastical elements use *exaggerated natural motifs*: bioluminescent moss, shimmering chitin, glowing runes
- Lighting driven by time-of-day and environmental storytelling (e.g., misty forests, golden-hour ruins)

**🧱 Texture/Material Direction:**

- **Physically Based Rendering (PBR)** for all materials
- Dynamic wear, aging, grime, moss, rust, and dirt buildup based on in-game systems (e.g., use, weather, neglect)
- Integration of **AI-assisted texture generation** and procedural surface detailing (Substance Designer + Megascans + custom AI tools)
