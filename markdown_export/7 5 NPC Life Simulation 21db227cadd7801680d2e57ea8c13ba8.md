# 7.5 NPC Life Simulation

NPCs are more than static dialogue sources — they live, age, remember, and adapt.

- **Needs & Goals**: Hunger, sleep, relationships, ambition, loyalty.
- **Memory & Consequences**: NPCs recall player actions and gossip spreads regionally.
- **Routine Variability**: Day/night schedules, job cycles, seasonal behaviors.
- **TTRPG Comparison**: Inspired by GM-run characters with evolving backstories, player interaction logs, and dynamic faction responses.
