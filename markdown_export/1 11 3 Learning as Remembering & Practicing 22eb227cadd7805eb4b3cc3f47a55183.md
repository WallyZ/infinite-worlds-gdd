# 1.11.3 Learning as Remembering & Practicing

### **Overview**

In *Infinite Worlds*, the learning process is not about gaining abilities from nothing—it's about the **player coming to understand what their character already knows**, and practicing those abilities in meaningful, immersive ways. This creates a powerful alignment between *player skill* and *character knowledge*, reinforcing the one-life-per-character design and promoting thoughtful mastery over reckless trial and error.
