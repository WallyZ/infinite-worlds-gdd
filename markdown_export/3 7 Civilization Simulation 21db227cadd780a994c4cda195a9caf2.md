# 3.7 Civilization Simulation

Civilizations evolve in response to their environment, technology, and social developments.

- **Cultural Development**: Each civilization has language, religion, architecture, technology, and alliances.
- **Rise and Fall**: Simulated across millennia — players may explore ruins, learn ancient languages, or witness rebirth.
- **Faction Interaction**: NPC factions have goals, memory, and evolving relationships with others and the player.
- **TTRPG Parallel**: Mirrors long-term campaign tracking tools like domain-level play in *AD&D* or GM world calendars in *Pendragon*.
