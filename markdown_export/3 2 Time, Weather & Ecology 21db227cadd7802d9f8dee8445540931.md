# 3.2 Time, Weather & Ecology

Time influences both the world and its inhabitants in profound ways.

- **Seasons**: Affect agriculture, behavior, migration, and availability of resources.
- High and Low Tides
- **Calendars**: Civilizations track time with distinct calendars, festivals, and holidays.
- **Celestial Mechanics**: Moon phases, eclipses, star alignments may affect psionics, magic, or myth.
- **TTRPG Connection**: Campaign calendars (e.g., *Pendragon’s* seasonal play), moon phase-based spellcasting, or divine omens.
