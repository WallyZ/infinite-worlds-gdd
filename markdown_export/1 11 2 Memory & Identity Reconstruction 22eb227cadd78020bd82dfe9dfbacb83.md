# 1.11.2 Memory & Identity Reconstruction

### **Overview**

In *Infinite Worlds*, the player steps into the shoes of a fully-formed adult character with a rich background, established skillset, and personal philosophy. However, **the player is not yet familiar** with what this character knows—creating a compelling dynamic of *learning through doing*, *reflection*, and *guided revelation*.

This system is not about uncovering forgotten memories but about **bridging the gap between player and character knowledge**. It emphasizes experiential understanding, natural discovery, and immersive intuition over explicit tutorials or stat sheets.

---

### **Key Principles**

- **The character knows things the player does not.**
- **Nothing is forgotten—only unrecognized by the player.**
- **The world presents opportunities to recontextualize that knowledge naturally.**
- **There are no "flashback unlocks"—skills are proven through practice.**
