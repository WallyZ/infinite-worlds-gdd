### 🧭 **Short Description (Steam Standard – 1–2 Sentences):**

*Infinite Worlds* is a VR-first sandbox RPG set in a fully simulated, procedurally generated fantasy world the size of Earth. Survive, explore, and shape a living realm where every action leaves a lasting legacy—and each life is only lived once.

---

### 🧙‍♂️ **Long Description (Steam Overview Section):**

**There are no quest markers. No second lives. Just you, your mind, and a world that breathes with or without you.**

*Infinite Worlds* is an immersive sandbox RPG built from the ground up for VR, with full flat-screen support. Explore a procedurally generated, Earth-scale fantasy world teeming with dynamic ecosystems, evolving NPC civilizations, and ancient secrets. Every mountain, ruin, and culture is unique to your world.

There’s no handholding—players master real-world skills like foraging, astronomy, and metalwork to survive. Speak spells with your voice, wield weapons with real motion, and navigate cultures and spirits that remember your choices. NPCs have emotions, memories, and personal goals. Death is permanent—but your story echoes forward.

Whether playing solo in a local world you can pause or online in a persistent multiplayer simulation, *Infinite Worlds* offers a living experience where **every decision matters**, **every interaction is physical**, and **every world is uniquely yours**.

---

### 🧩 **Key Features:**

- 🌍 **Earth-Scale Procedural World**
    
    Explore a massive, seamless planet with continents, biomes, weather systems, and cultures generated fresh each playthrough.
    
- 🧠 **Real Skill-Based Progression**
    
    Learn through experience—not menus. Identify herbs, read the stars, or forge blades the way you would in real life.
    
- 🧬 **Living Ecosystems & Civilizations**
    
    Everything is simulated—from predator-prey dynamics to NPC politics and generational memory.
    
- 🗡️ **VR-First Physical Interaction**
    
    Swing swords, stir potions, build with your hands, and cast spells with gestures and spoken incantations.
    
- 🌀 **Voice, Gesture & Focus-Based Magic**
    
    Unlock mystical powers using hand-drawn runes, spoken words, and psionic concentration—with risks as real as the rewards.
    
- 👁️ **No HUD, No Handholding**
    
    Diegetic interfaces, organic tutorials, and meaningful discovery replace traditional UI and quest systems.
    
- 👻 **Persistent Legacy & Permanent Death**
    
    Characters die forever, but their choices ripple through generations—through myths, ruins, descendants, or curses.
    
- 🌐 **Solo or Persistent Multiplayer**
    
    Play offline with full simulation, or join a multiplayer world that evolves even when you're away.
