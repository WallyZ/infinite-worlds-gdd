# 5.2.1 Combat AI Logic

### **Overview:**

Enemies in *Infinite Worlds* use emergent tactics. They don’t just charge blindly; they **observe, coordinate, adapt, and retreat**.

- **Group Tactics:**
    - Flanking, focus fire, suppression, cover usage.
- **Emotive Reactions:**
    - Enemies panic, become enraged, seek revenge, or flee.
- **Learning AI:**
    - High-level foes adapt to repeated strategies.
- **Social Signals:**
    - Allies may call out threats or distract foes.
