# 5.2 Combat AI & Reactive Behaviors

NPC companions demonstrate **emergent, personality-driven behavior** during combat, making each encounter dynamic and unpredictable. Their decisions are influenced by internal attributes such as bravery, intelligence, loyalty, and selfishness.

**Key Features:**

- **Situational Reactions:**
    - Brave characters **initiate flanks**, charge into danger, or taunt enemies.
    - Cautious or survival-focused allies **seek cover**, provide overwatch, or guard key points.
    - Cowardly NPCs might **flee**, **freeze**, or **bargain** if overwhelmed.
- **Relationship-Based Prioritization:**
    - Strong bonds influence **protective behavior**—a companion may risk their life to shield someone they care about.
    - Conflicted or resentful relationships might lead to hesitation, **inaction**, or even **passive sabotage**.
- **Class & Personality Influence:**
    - A **selfish rogue** may loot corpses mid-battle, prioritizing treasure over teamwork.
    - A **disciplined knight** follows formation tactics and chain-of-command protocols.
    - A **vengeful character** may break formation to pursue a specific enemy.
- **Learning Over Time:**
    - NPCs adapt based on the player’s tactics; a frequently flanking player might inspire others to mirror aggressive playstyles.
