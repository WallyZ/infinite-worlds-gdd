# 1.3 Death & Permanence: One Life, Echoing Through Eternity

In *Infinite Worlds*, life is sacred not because it lasts forever — but because it leaves **permanent echoes** in the world, in others, and in the stories that follow. When a character dies, the memory of their actions, relationships, and sacrifices persist. The world remembers.
This system transforms death from punishment into **meaningful presence**, making *how you live* and *how you affect others* more valuable than winning or surviving.
