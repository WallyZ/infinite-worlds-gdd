# 4.6.4 Gameplay Mechanics in VR

### Controls & Interface:

- **No HUDs** — all information through:
    - Astral compass globe (holographic VR prop)
    - Runes glowing along the helm
    - Changes in ambient audio or starlight pulses
- **Manual Rune Input:** Carve runes physically into console panels to activate travel functions (direction, shield, acceleration)

### Travel System:

| Layer | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Local Realm Layer** | Navigating around the planet’s exosphere or moons |
| **Aether Veil** | Deep travel across sky currents; harder to navigate |
| **Planar Threads** | Hidden dimensional routes; require dreamwalking or soul-travel |
| **Celestial Landing** | Piloting down to surface through turbulence and gravity wells |
