# 5.1.3 Psionic Combat

### **Overview:**

Psionics focus on **mind-over-matter** abilities. They require mental discipline, emotional control, and are less about flashy effects than about manipulating perception, thoughts, and behavior.

**Key Systems:**

- **Offensive Psionics:**
    - Induce fear, confusion, hallucinations.
    - Psychic blasts or mind-piercing strikes.
- **Control & Domination:**
    - Possession, charm, command—resisted by mental fortitude.
    - NPCs may react differently to visible vs invisible manipulation.
- **Defensive Psionics:**
    - Mind shields, thought cloaking, emotion suppression.
- **Psionic Stamina & Strain:**
    - Repeated use causes mental fatigue, nosebleeds, migraines.
    - Overuse can lead to loss of control or permanent side effects.
- **Detection & Sensitivity:**
    - Some NPCs (like mind-sensitive species) can detect psionic pulses or intentions.
