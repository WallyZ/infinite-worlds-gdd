# 7.3.5 Companion & Ally Systems

- Each companion has:
    - **Personal questlines** unlocked through deepening connection.
    - **Dynamic influence meters** (partially visible or hinted).
- Social interactions include:
    - **Persuasion, intimidation, humor, empathy** — enhanced by skills or emotional state.
    - **Shared rituals** (songs, meals, games) that strengthen bonds.
- Bonding and betrayal mechanics
- Private conversation memories
- Internal desires vs. outward loyalty
- NPCs actively **talk among themselves** — gossiping, bonding, arguing, grieving.
- Social alignment affects **group cohesion** — loners may isolate, extroverts may smooth tensions.
- Relationships shift over time through **shared experiences**, betrayals, successes, and failures.

### 💔 Conflict, Betrayal & Departure:

- If you **violate a companion’s values** or neglect their needs, they may:
    - Confront you.
    - Demand an apology or justification.
    - Abandon or betray you — especially if aligned with a rival faction.
