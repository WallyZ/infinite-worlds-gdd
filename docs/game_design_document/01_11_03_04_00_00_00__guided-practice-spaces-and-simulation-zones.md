# 1.11.3.4 Guided Practice Spaces & Simulation Zones

To support the one-life rule, players are given **safe opportunities to test and refine their skills** without fatal consequences.

These include:

- **Physical dojos or shrines** where repetition and mastery are honored.
- **Dream-state simulations** invoked through rituals or meditation, where players reenact past trials.
- **Mentor-guided drills** which simulate intense experiences (e.g., silent hunting, fire evocation, water breathing) in controlled environments.

These spaces are:

- Diegetic (in-world, not menu-based).
- Optional but rewarding for careful players.
- Thematically relevant (e.g., ancient memory domes or ancestor-echo chambers).
