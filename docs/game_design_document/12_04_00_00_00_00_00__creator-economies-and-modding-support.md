# 12.4 Creator Economies & Modding Support

### Overview

*Infinite Worlds* doesn’t just allow modding — it embraces it as a core part of the ecosystem. This section outlines a **creator-first economy** that supports modders with visibility, monetization, in-game integration, and protection of their work, while maintaining fairness, player freedom, and immersion.

---

### 🧑‍🎨 **Creator Roles & Tiers**

| Tier | Access & Perks | Ideal For |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Explorer** | Free access to create and share public mods | Hobbyists, open-source creators |
| **Artisan** | Verified creator status, in-game visibility, analytics | Serious modders, small teams |
| **Guilded** | Monetized mod storefronts, seasonal rewards, Discord perks | Studio-backed or semi-pro creators |

---

### 🛒 **Player-to-Player Mod Marketplace (Optional)**

- **Free & Paid Mod Support**
    - Players can browse, download, rate, and optionally **support modders financially**
    - Monetization is **opt-in** and balanced to preserve the game’s creative spirit
    - Paid mods must pass a **Quality & Compatibility Review** (can be community-driven)
- **Revenue Sharing**
    - Modders retain **70–90%** of revenue depending on distribution platform
    - Integrated Patreon, Ko-fi, or itch.io support for external linking and funding
- **In-Game Diegetic Storefront (Optional)**
    - Magical tome or archive inside the world (e.g., "Library of Echoes") where players discover featured mods
    - Purchases or downloads framed as scrolls, visions, or unlockable relics

---

### 🛠️ **Support for Monetized Creators**

- **Creator Dashboards**
    - Analytics on downloads, feedback, compatibility issues, donations
    - Version control and rollback support for live patches
- **Anti-Piracy & Attribution**
    - Optional watermarking and version-locking
    - Code/asset signature checks prevent unauthorized repackaging
- **Curation & Spotlight Programs**
    - Monthly showcases for high-quality or lore-friendly content
    - In-game events tied to featured mods (e.g., “Mod of the Month” becomes a world event or quest)

---

### 🤝 **Community & Legal Guidelines**

- **Open License Framework**
    - Encourages permissive sharing, but creators can specify license (MIT, CC-BY, etc.)
    - Shared library of community-made reusable assets and logic components
- **Dispute Resolution & Mediation**
    - Community moderators and dev support assist in disputes (e.g., plagiarism claims, stolen mods)
- **Mod Certification**
    - "Lore-Compatible," "Multiplayer-Ready," and "Sandbox-Safe" tags show compatibility and roleplay fitness

---

### 🎁 **Seasonal Rewards & Creator Incentives**

- **Modder Festivals**: Community-voted mod events where top entries are featured globally
- **Developer Support Tokens**: Devs can directly grant rewards or recognition to impactful modders
- **Game World Integration Rewards**:
    - Mods voted into canon through player governance
    - Winners receive custom NPCs, lore references, or locations based on their content

---

### 📦 **Bundling & Collaborative Packs**

- **Mod Packs & World Templates**
    - Bundled themed packs (e.g., "Witchwood Realms," "Crystalpunk Overhaul")
    - Creators can team up to publish compatible world-gen presets with curated mods
- **Shared Revenue Splits**
    - Optional smart-contract-based or platform-based revenue sharing for team projects

---

### 🌎 **Cross-Platform Creator Tools**

- **Mod Export for Multiple Stores**
    - One-click publishing for Steam Workshop, Nexus Mods, and internal mod browser
    - Unified tagging and metadata standards
- **Creator Mode on Standalone VR (Planned)**
    - Allow lightweight mod uploads from Quest or portable devices via companion PC client

---

This mod economy supports hobbyists, educators, and professionals alike — encouraging sustainable, ethical, and community-driven content while **keeping the world of *Infinite Worlds* alive and evolving far beyond the base game.**

- Share-revenue for top modders or tool builders
- Fan fiction canon integration events
- Mod-based contest system with lore importation
