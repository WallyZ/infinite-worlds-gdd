# 8.6 VFX & Particle Systems

**🔮 Magic & Psionics FX:**

- Niagara-based spell effects react to *gesture speed, energy type, casting accuracy, and user class*
- Each spell includes subtle *personalization*—AI alters color, intensity, or after-image based on player behavior and focus
- Psionics use *aura-like fields* with animated thought-patterns or emotional ripples

**⚔️ Combat & World Impact FX:**

- Real-time particle FX for strikes, impacts, spell explosions, environmental breakage
- Ground deformation, scorch marks, snow plowing, acid burn, etc., tied to force and substance type
- Dynamic lighting interacts with fog, particles, and surfaces for spelllight and torch-casting effects

**🌧️ Dynamic Environmental FX:**

- Reactive VFX tied to world simulation: wind patterns, pollen bursts, seasonal storms, ash from fires
- Localized events (e.g., forest fire) alter regional lighting, VFX density, and skybox hues
