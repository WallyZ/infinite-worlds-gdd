# 5.1.2 Magic Combat

### **Overview:**

Magic is immersive and diegetic. Players use **gestures, voice incantations**, and **mental focus** to wield arcane power. Spells must be discovered, learned, and refined. Overuse or carelessness may have physical, mental, or world-altering consequences.

**Key Systems:**

- **Casting Methods:**
    - Gestural casting via VR hand movements.
    - Voice-recognition incantations (tone, pronunciation matter).
    - Runic casting (drawn or placed glyphs).
- **Spell Categories:**
    - Direct damage (firebolts, lightning)
    - Battlefield control (barriers, knockbacks)
    - Area effects (fog, firestorms)
    - Utility (light, teleport, heal)
    - Summoning or binding creatures.
- **Resources & Costs:**
    - Mana, focus, reagents, or mental strain.
    - Longer or more powerful spells have higher preparation and cost.
- **Disruption & Countermagic:**
    - Casting can be interrupted by taking damage or losing focus.
    - Anti-magic fields, wards, or reflexive counterspells exist.
- **Environmental Interaction:**
    - Fire spells can ignite flammable areas.
    - Water-based spells can douse or electrify.
    - Terrain may shape or be reshaped by magic (e.g., earth spikes).
