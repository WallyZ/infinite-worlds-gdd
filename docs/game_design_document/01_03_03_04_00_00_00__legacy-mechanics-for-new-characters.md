# 1.3.3.4 Legacy Mechanics for New Characters

When starting a new life:

- May inherit:
    - **Skills, curses, boons** from prior life (only if tied to world memory)
    - **Inherited quests**, like finishing the rebellion you started
    - **Visions or dreams** based on what happened last time

> Optional spiritual lore: The soul fragments but leaves resonance behind. Sometimes, it whispers.
>
