# 3.10.14.2 Navigators, Wayfinding & Spell Helms

### **Travel Mechanics**

- **Spelljamming Helms**: Enchanted thrones linking a mage’s mind to the ship; drains mana, essence, or memory
- **Navigation**:
    - Based on known **waypoints**, **myths**, or **ritual coordinates**
    - **Navigation errors** can send players into Far Realm echoes, dreamfolds, or dead gods
- **Speed**:
    - Normal movement = weeks of travel across dream-space
    - **Planar currents** or shortcut rituals reduce time, with risk
