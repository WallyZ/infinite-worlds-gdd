### 🤖 NPCs & Social Simulation

**AI-Driven Characters**

- NPCs have persistent:
    - **Memory** of events (per character)
    - **Schedules** (e.g., farming, guard patrol, market visit)
    - **Moods** (e.g., grudge, admiration, fatigue)
    - **Relationships** (alliances, betrayals, family, lovers)
- NPCs form friendships, rivalries, factions, and ideologies over time.

**Companions & Party Dynamics**

- Companions aren't static followers:
    - React emotionally to choices (approval/disapproval)
    - Can form relationships with each other (romance, rivalries)
    - May leave, betray, or protect the player based on trust level
- Dynamic party banter, needs (sleep, food, injury), and interjections during quests

**Faction & Reputation System**

- Reputation tracked at:
    - **Personal** (by individual)
    - **Regional** (towns, cities)
    - **Factional** (kingdoms, religions, guilds)
- Choices ripple outward:
    - Support rebels and be exiled from cities
    - Save a child and be honored by their family line
    - Steal and leave behind a legend of infamy
