# 12.2.3 Patreon

### 💫 **Patreon Pitch for *Infinite Worlds***

### 🧭 What is *Infinite Worlds*?

*Infinite Worlds* is a VR-first, first-person sandbox RPG set in a procedurally generated, Earth-sized fantasy world that **lives, grows, and remembers**. There are no quest markers. No static NPCs. No handholding. Instead, players learn by doing—crafting, surviving, and shaping a story that unfolds through **real physics, real discovery, and real consequence**.

It's a game about forging your own path in a living, breathing realm where **every creature, culture, and system evolves over time**. From forging blades with your hands to casting spells with your voice and gestures, *Infinite Worlds* is about *immersive interaction, emergent storytelling,* and a deep, persistent world that continues even after you're gone.

---

### 🛠 Why Patreon?

This is more than just a game—it's a long-term creative vision.

*Infinite Worlds* is being built **independently and transparently**, with a strong focus on community feedback, player-driven systems, and meaningful worldbuilding. Your support allows us to:

- Build ambitious, simulation-driven systems without compromise
- Expand our team with passionate artists, designers, and AI specialists
- Share early builds, devlogs, design docs, and behind-the-scenes content
- Create a space where **supporters shape the world alongside us**

We’re not just making a game—we’re building a platform for exploration, storytelling, and creativity at a massive scale.

---

### 🎁 What You'll Get

By joining us on Patreon, you'll gain:

- **Early access** to dev builds, concepts, and experiments
- **Exclusive insights** into our design philosophy, worldbuilding, and mechanics
- **Behind-the-scenes devlogs**, livestreams, and design discussions
- **Your name or creations** immortalized in-world (depending on tier)
- A chance to help shape the direction of *Infinite Worlds* from the ground up

### 💰 Monetization Strategy

Primary model: **Patreon Support**

Supporters receive exclusive perks to deepen engagement without compromising fairness or gameplay balance:

- **Regular Updates** – Behind-the-scenes insights, dev logs, milestone previews
- **Exclusive Content** – Concept art, design notes, and early builds
- **Customization Rewards** – Unique skins, avatars, or in-game flair for patrons
- **Community Access** – Private Discord/forum with voting rights and developer chats
- **Early Access** – Playtest alpha builds and expansions before public release
- **Supporter Recognition** – Credits, named NPCs, or livestream shoutouts
- **Bonus VR Experiences** – Exclusive mini-games or narrative vignettes for patrons
