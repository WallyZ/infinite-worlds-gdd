# 8.1.3 Visual Tone & Art Style

- **Overall Look:** Painterly realism with soft edges. Texture-rich environments. Stylized realism similar to *Morrowind*, *NaissanceE*, *Ashen*, or *Dark Souls 1*.
- **Lighting:** Natural, dynamic, atmospheric. Use god rays, mist, torchlight flicker. Shadows play a key role.
- **Textures:** Weathered, tactile, handcrafted—wood grain, moss, dust, rust, bone.
- **UI Style:** Minimal, diegetic when possible. Inscriptional symbols over traditional buttons. Floating parchment UI for menus.
- **Motion:** Slow, weighty gestures. Realistic physical feedback. Fluid wind, smoke, spirit trails.

## World Mood, References, and VR Considerations

- World mood should combine dreamlike realism, emotional color theory, painterly texture, physical detail, magical distortion, planar distortion, non-Euclidean logic, shifting hues, and surreal glow.
- Key visual references include real-world nature, spiritual artwork, ancient architecture, mythic symbols, Moebius, Studio Ghibli, Shadow of the Colossus, Nausicaä, and similar mythic-natural works.
- VR presentation should prioritize readability, eye comfort, strong object silhouettes in 3D, and a stylization-vs-realism balance that reduces fatigue.
