# 4.6 Astral Navigation & Celestial Runeships

> “To sail the stars is to read the soul of the world — not through force, but through resonance, will, and ancient understanding.”
>

---

### 🌌 OVERVIEW

Space travel in *Infinite Worlds* is not technological, but metaphysical. Players access other celestial bodies, planes, and sky-realms through the fusion of:

- **Astral Navigation**: The art of aligning one's soul, will, and vessel with the currents of the astral sea.
- **Celestial Runeships**: Magical vessels forged from starbound materials and bound by sacred geometry and alchemical law.
- **Spelljammer Inspirations**: Vessels glide through astral and etheric currents, surrounded by their own breathable air and governed by mental/spiritual focus and arcane resonance.

This system enables **non-technological space travel** that still demands **realistic learning, preparation, and danger**, aligning with your skill-as-knowledge design.
