# 3.9 Magical Ecology & Anomalies

Magic and psionics aren’t abstract forces — they have real environmental and systemic impact.

- **Ley Lines & Nodes**: Energy flows across the world affect magic intensity and outcomes.
- **Contamination or Taint**: Overuse or misuse of magic can alter landscapes or mutate creatures.
- **Localized Effects**: Psionic overuse might create dream-zones, memory anomalies, or mind-linked flora/fauna.
- **TTRPG Echo**: Found in systems like *Ars Magica* or *Dark Sun*, where magical resources and corruption change biomes.
