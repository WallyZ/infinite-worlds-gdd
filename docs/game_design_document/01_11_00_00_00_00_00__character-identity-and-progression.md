# 1.11 Character Identity & Progression

### **Overview**

Character identity in *Infinite Worlds* is not defined by stats and perks alone, but by lived memory, learned skills, belief systems, and psychological conditioning. The player must not only inhabit a character but also come to understand what that character knows, what they’ve been taught, and how they see the world.

This system is designed to ensure:

- Players are **never surprised by arbitrary failure**—they have the chance to train, study, or seek knowledge beforehand.
- **All in-world information** is *relevant*, *learnable*, and *discoverable*.
- **One-life permanence** means identity and growth matter; learning is legacy.
