# 4.6.2 Celestial Runeships (The Vessel)

> Each Runeship is a unique, semi-living construct attuned to astral forces and built with ritual precision.
>

**Crafting Requirements:**

- **Starmetal hull** (harvested during meteor showers or from lunar forges)
- **Ley-bound core** (crafted from planet-aligned crystals)
- **Runic lattice** (player-drawn in VR, affects handling & capabilities)
- **Soul anchor** (player’s essence or familiar bound to ship)

**Variants:**

| Type | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| **Wyrm-Shell Glider** | Fast but fragile, good for scouting. |
| **Obsidian Leviathan** | Heavily fortified for voidstorms or planar incursions. |
| **Lucent Reed Skiff** | Built from living astral flora, ideal for dream navigation. |

### Features:

- Gravity and breathable air via soulfield envelope
- Responds to pilot’s emotional state, making player mindset and intent matter
- Requires **co-pilot or navigator** for longer journeys
