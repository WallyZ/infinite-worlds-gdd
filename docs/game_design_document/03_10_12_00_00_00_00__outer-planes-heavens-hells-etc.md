# 3.10.12 Outer Planes (Heavens, Hells, etc.)

## *(aligned with morality, belief, and the afterlife)*

These are abstract realms tied to **gods, philosophies, and alignment**.

- **Heavenly / Good-aligned Planes**
    - **Elysium**, **Mount Celestia**, **Bytopia**
    - Peaceful afterlives, divine beauty, harmony
- **Chaotic Good**
    - **Arborea** (Olympian), seat of elven gods and passion
- **Neutral / Balanced**
    - **Mechanus** (pure law), **Outlands** (spokes of neutrality)
- **Evil-aligned Planes**
    - **Abyss** (chaotic evil), **Baator** (lawful evil Hell), **Carceri**, **Hades**
    - Full of demons, devils, punishment, domination

These planes often host the **afterlives of mortals**, the homes of **gods**, and are the source of **divine and infernal magic**.
