# 7.1.7 Environmental Storytelling & Social Echoes

- Ruins, settlements, and forgotten places **hint at past social dynamics**: failed rebellions, unrequited love, betrayal.
- The world reacts to player actions:
    - Towns whisper of your deeds.
    - Children imitate your behavior.
    - Animals respond to aura or emotional tone.
- Companion reactions to locations/events create **layered social feedback**.
- Traces of past events: torn tents, blood trails, burned meals
- Shared memory: “The last time the guards came, they left us no food.”
