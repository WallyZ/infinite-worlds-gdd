# 1.11.3.1 Knowledge is Present, Not Gated

All core skills the character "knows" are **available from the beginning**, but their use depends on:

- **Contextual triggers** that reveal familiarity (e.g., picking up a tool and instinctively holding it properly).
- **Player observation and experimentation** that allow rediscovery of technique (e.g., mixing known ingredients in a way that creates a predictable result).
- **Mentor references or narrative clues** that offer interpretive cues without overt explanation.

> Example: Your character may have known a regional martial art in their youth. The player doesn't need to unlock it—they just need to choose to practice with a staff, and the correct grip and movement patterns emerge in muscle memory.
>
