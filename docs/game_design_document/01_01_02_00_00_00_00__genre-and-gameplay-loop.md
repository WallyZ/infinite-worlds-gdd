# 1.1.2 Genre & Gameplay Loop

### 🎮 **Primary Genres**

- VR Immersive Simulation
- Fantasy Survival Sandbox
- First-Person Exploration / Legacy RPG
- Procedural World & Culture Simulation

---

### 🔄 **Gameplay Loop Summary**

> You awaken alone in a vast, unfamiliar land. No quests. No map markers. Just your wits, your senses, and a living world that doesn't wait for you.
>

**Moment-to-moment gameplay:**

- Use your body and intuition: climb, swim, forage, hunt, and craft.
- Observe the world’s physical rules and social patterns—learn through action, not abstraction.
- Build tools, shelter, and relationships. Decode languages, tame creatures, and uncover ancient lore.
- Interact with NPCs through gesture, voice, and reputation—your actions define trust, fear, and memory.
- React to seasonal shifts, hunger, illness, and unseen magical forces.

**Long-term progression:**

- As you master systems and skills, your actions shape settlements, myths, and social structures.
- Death is permanent—but your character’s deeds ripple outward, influencing the world across time.
- Return as a new character in the same persistent world, changed by past choices.
- Reclaim forgotten knowledge, seek out echoes of your past life, or confront consequences you set in motion generations ago.

> Infinite Worlds is not just about survival—it’s about significance.
>
>
> One life. Infinite echoes.
>
