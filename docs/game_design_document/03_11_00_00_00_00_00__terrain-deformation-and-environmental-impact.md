# 3.11 Terrain Deformation & Environmental Impact

The environment is not static and responds to natural forces and player actions.

- **Player Influence**: Digging, building, deforestation, and over-gathering alter the landscape and ecosystems.
- **Weathering & Erosion**: Long-term natural forces slowly change terrain.
- **Magical Effects**: Spells or artifacts may warp or corrupt regions (e.g., blighted forests or floating shards).
- **Tabletop Analogy**: Echoes domain building or resource depletion mechanics in sandbox TTRPG campaigns.
