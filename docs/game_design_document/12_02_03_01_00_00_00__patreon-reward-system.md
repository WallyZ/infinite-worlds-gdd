# 12.2.3.1 Patreon Reward System

## 🧱 Patreon Reward System for *Infinite Worlds*

---

### 💡 Best Practices from Similar Campaigns

**Successful indie Patreon campaigns usually offer:**

- Early access to builds and dev updates (even if just videos or mechanics breakdowns)
- High transparency on development process
- Lore drops or concept art previews
- Community polls or dev Q&As
- Name recognition (credits, in-game, etc.)
- Very limited (if any) physical rewards to avoid overhead

For *Infinite Worlds*, we’ll lean into immersive digital experiences, design transparency, and community involvement — **let supporters feel like founding members of a living world**.

---

## 🏆 Tiered Rewards

(Feel free to rename the tiers with lore-appropriate or in-world names later—e.g. *Wanderer*, *Seer*, *Pathfinder*, etc.)

---

### 🌱 **Tier 1 — $3/month**

**“The Ember”**

*Supporter tier for those who believe in the vision and want to stay in the loop.*

**Rewards:**

- ✉️ Monthly dev updates with behind-the-scenes insights
- 📸 Access to early screenshots, concept art, and lore drops
- 🎧 Patreon-only Q&A polls or audio posts
- 🏷️ Name in the *Supporter Hall* on the official website

---

### 🔍 **Tier 2 — $7/month**

**“The Observer”**

*For lore-hunters and curious minds who want deeper access.*

Includes all previous rewards, plus:

- 📜 Early access to design documents, GDD sections, and devlogs
- 🧪 Private demo access when available (visual walkthroughs, mechanics showcases, etc.)
- 🧠 Participation in quarterly worldbuilding polls (e.g., “What kind of ancient civilization might exist in this world?”)
- 💬 Private Discord role with access to dev discussion channels

---

### ⚒️ **Tier 3 — $15/month**

**“The Crafter”**

*For those who want to help shape the world itself.*

Includes all previous rewards, plus:

- 🏗️ Access to early playable systems test builds (as available)
- 🎨 Voting on visual style decisions, creature design, or feature prioritization
- 🏛️ Your name (or username) added to in-game content: a book, carving, or memorial stone
- 📚 Access to exclusive behind-the-scenes videos (e.g., gesture magic dev, ecosystem design breakdowns)

---

### 🔮 **Tier 4 — $25/month**

**“The Shaper”**

*Worldbuilders and patrons who want a seat at the table.*

Includes all previous rewards, plus:

- 💬 Monthly design livestream or voice chat with Q&A
- 🖋️ Ability to submit world ideas (e.g., myths, locations, artifacts) that may be included in-game
- 🌐 Voting access for multiplayer testing or Ethereal Plane expansions
- 🧾 “Lore Spotlight”: Get credited for inspiring a specific item, region, or mechanic

---

### 👑 **Tier 5 — $50/month**

**“The Echo”** *(limited slots)*

*For those who wish to leave a permanent mark.*

Includes all previous rewards, plus:

- 🏛️ Custom in-game NPC, tombstone, book, or landmark named or inspired by you
- 🗺️ Invitation to private “Worldforge” test server during persistent world builds
- 🎥 Personalized video updates every quarter from the dev team
- 📜 Lifetime place in “Founding Patrons” credits in the game and website

---

## 🎯 Optional Stretch Goals / Milestones

To incentivize growth:

- **$250/mo** – Unlock dev podcast with lore deep-dives and simulation theory
- **$500/mo** – Open community writing or creature design jam
- **$1,000/mo** – Hire freelance creature or environment artist for additional biome set
- **$2,000/mo** – Fund persistent multiplayer test phase
- **$5,000/mo** – Begin monthly major content reveals or cinematic trailer development

## 🧙‍♂️ Bonus Ideas

- **“Myth Seed” tier (1-time $75)** – Submit a myth, rune, or story fragment that could appear in-game (once per supporter)
- **Founder’s Discord** – Tier 3+ get early invites to Founders-only playtests
- **Legacy Credit** – Tier 4+ patrons get immortalized as a line in ancient history books or tomb ruins
