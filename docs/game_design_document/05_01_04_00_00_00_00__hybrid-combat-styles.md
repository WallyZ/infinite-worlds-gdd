# 5.1.4 Hybrid Combat Styles

### **Overview:**

These styles combine aspects of physical, magical, and psionic systems for unique, high-skillplay combatants.

- **Spellblades & Rune Warriors:**
    - Combine melee strikes with quick-cast spells or enchanted weaponry.
- **Mind-Mages:**
    - Blend psionic suggestion with spell shaping for enhanced control.
- **Beast or Mount Combat:**
    - Combat while riding or directing companion creatures.
- **Environmental Combat:**
    - Use terrain, traps, fire, water flow, avalanche hazards, and elevation tactically.
- **Ritual Combat:**
    - Long-prepared spells or psionic fields that affect areas slowly or with powerful results.
- **Crafted Combat:**
    - Construct and deploy traps, gadgets, or alchemical bombs during battle.
