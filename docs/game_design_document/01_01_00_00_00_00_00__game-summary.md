# 1.1 Game Summary

**Infinite Worlds** is a **first-person, VR-first sandbox RPG** set in a fully simulated, Earth-scale fantasy realm. It combines **tactile immersion**, **deep simulation**, and **player-driven storytelling** to create a dynamic epic of survival, discovery, and legacy.

The world is governed by layered systems—**physics, biology, ecology, memory, emotion, and culture**—that function with or without the player. There are no quest markers, no stat dumps, no artificial UI guidance. Instead, players learn as their character would: through **trial and error**, **in-world experimentation**, **mentorship**, and the **language of experience**.

Your journey begins as a single life in an uncharted world, but your choices echo far beyond that life. Whether you craft a bow, speak with a ghost, or die defending a village, **the world remembers.** Past characters may return as **spirits, legends, teachers, or cursed echoes**, influencing both the landscape and future generations.

Progress is rooted in **real knowledge and physical mastery**—from **identifying edible plants** to **navigating by starlight**, **forging tools from raw ore**, or **channeling magic through ritual and gesture**. Every object, creature, and structure can be learned, shaped, and understood if the player pays attention.

You can play in **single-player pauseable mode**, or on a **persistent multiplayer world** shared with friends or strangers. In either mode, your character's life is finite—but your story may outlive you.

At its core, *Infinite Worlds* is a place where **the intimate and the epic intertwine**, where courage, curiosity, and consequence forge stories that live on across simulated centuries.
